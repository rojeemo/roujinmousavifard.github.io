Innovative Safety Device for the Food Industry

We developed an innovative safety device tailored for the food industry, designed to protect operators working in high-risk environments, specifically in gum production lines. The system addresses the critical safety challenge of operators manually feeding raw materials into a grinder, where proximity to the blade poses a severe hazard.

The device comprises two interconnected components:

  • Smart Bracelet: Worn by the operator, this bracelet continuously monitors the distance between the operator's hand and the grinder blade.
  • Grinder Safety Module: Integrated into the grinder, this module communicates with the bracelet to ensure real-time safety measures.

If the operator's hand comes too close to the blade, the system immediately shuts down the grinder, preventing potential accidents and ensuring a safe working environment. This innovative solution combines precise proximity sensing with rapid response capabilities, offering unparalleled protection for operators while maintaining workflow efficiency.

By prioritizing safety without compromising productivity, this device exemplifies how technology can address critical challenges in the food industry, setting a new standard for workplace safety in hazardous environments.

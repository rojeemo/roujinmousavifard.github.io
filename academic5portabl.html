Portable Vibration Simulation System

Design and Development of a Portable Vibration Simulation System A compact and versatile vibration simulation system capable of generating vibrations in the range of 5 Hz to 20 kHz. The system is designed to handle up to a 2 kg mass, ensuring robust and reliable performance across a variety of testing scenarios. An integrated software interface was developed to visualize the generated waveforms in real time, providing users with enhanced control and monitoring capabilities for precise experimentation and analysis.

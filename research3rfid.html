RFID Reader with Tracking Module

Our Livestock Management System is a cutting-edge solution designed to transform livestock tracking and management. At its core are our advanced RFID readers and smart tags, enabling seamless identification and monitoring of animals across various critical parameters.

Our system integrates state-of-the-art passive and active RFID tags, available in underskin or bolus formats. These innovative tags monitor a wide range of vital data, including ID, temperature, pH levels, location, and estrus cycles, ensuring comprehensive health and performance tracking. The RFID readers provide reliable data transmission, enabling real-time monitoring and precise management.

The RFID reader device is equipped with a GPS module, allowing it to record the location of livestock and display it in the app alongside each animal’s profile. This feature ensures complete visibility into the whereabouts and status of every animal, streamlining operations and enhancing decision-making for livestock managers.

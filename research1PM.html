Predictive Maintenance System

The Predictive Maintenance System revolutionizes industrial machinery upkeep by predicting how long a machine can operate before maintenance is required and identifying specific components likely to fail, enabling targeted and efficient repairs. This capability reduces the need for frequent on-site visits, particularly in remote or hard-to-reach locations, where regular human inspections are challenging. By preventing unexpected downtime and enabling cost-effective maintenance, the system delivers a significant leap in Industry 4.0 maintenance technologies.

The custom-designed hardware supports multiple connectivity options, including 4G, LoRaWAN, BLE 5.0, and WiFi, ensuring seamless data transmission in even the most demanding industrial settings. It collects real-time vibration and performance data, which can be accessed via an intuitive online application for remote monitoring and analysis.

Available in two models—one powered by a built-in battery for portability and flexibility, and another connected to wired electricity for continuous operation—the system ensures adaptability to diverse operational environments.

Additionally, the system incorporates Augmented Reality (AR), offering a visual overlay of machinery components to highlight critical areas and provide actionable insights. This comprehensive solution combines advanced hardware and analytics, empowering industries to streamline their maintenance processes, minimize downtime, and optimize overall operational efficiency.

Modular Tracker Device

The Modular Tracker Device is a compact and versatile GPS tracking system designed for diverse applications, ranging from personal health monitoring to professional tracking in various environments. This device is characterized by its adaptability, precision, and user-centric design.

Physical Specifications

  • Dimensions: 40 × 30 × 9 mm.
  • Battery Life:
    • 90 hours at 20-second wake-up intervals.
    • Up to 2 years at 1-hour wake-up intervals.
    • 3 years in standby mode.
  • Durability: External antenna options for robust usage in vehicles.

Core Functionalities

Location Tracking

  • GPS/AGPS with multi-system support (Glonass, Beidou, Galileo).
  • Location accuracy within 2 meters.
  • Features: Geo-fencing, velocity tracking, altitude measurement, and distance monitoring.

Health Monitoring

  • Integrated sensors for heart rate, blood oxygen, temperature, blood pressure, humidity, and air quality.
  • Customizable health parameters with real-time alerts for abnormal values.

Communication

  • Supports GSM, GPRS, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and NFC.
  • Dual SIM capability for improved connectivity.
  • Includes a microphone, speaker, SOS button, and customizable volume settings.

User Interaction

  • LED indicators with customizable colors and patterns.
  • Wireless fast charging.
  • SOS functionality to send emergency location details via SMS or calls.

Health & Activity Monitoring

  • Tracks steps, walking distance, calories burned, sleep duration, and stress levels.
  • Advanced features such as scratching count alerts and customizable medicine schedules.

Software Features

  • Remote configuration for geo-fencing, tracking intervals, and light settings.
  • Firmware is upgradeable over-the-air (OTA).
  • Secure pairing with virtual leash functionality using Wi-Fi or Bluetooth.

Applications

  • Personal Use: Elderly care, pet monitoring, and fitness tracking.
  • Professional Use: Asset tracking, industrial monitoring, and medical applications.

This modular system integrates cutting-edge health and safety features with precise GPS tracking, making it adaptable for a wide range of industries and personal needs. Its robust design, advanced functionalities, and ease of customization set it apart as a pioneering solution in tracking technology.
